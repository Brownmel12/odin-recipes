Beans with Plantain

Description

    Ingredients

  • Beans
  • Red Oil
  • Riped palantain
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Gari
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Onions
  • Pepper
  • Tomatoes

    How to cook

  1. Wash your beans and place it in a saucepan with enough water and some salt to boil on your stove
  2. Wait for it to cook for 30-40 minutes. Make sure your stir from time to time
  3. While waiting for your beans, peel of some fingers of plantain and sprinkle a tablespon of table salt onit to taste
  4. Place your frying pan on fire with a considerate amount of vegetable oil
  5. Fry your plantain in the oil till it's reddish brown then you know it's ready
  6. Now place a small saucepan on the fire with some red oil
  7. Cut some onions into it and fry for a minute or two
  8. Blend some pepper with some onions and pour it into the red oil
  9. Add some salt to taste
  10. Slice some tomatoes into it and leave it to fry for some few minutes to fry
  11. Now take out you plate, scoop some beans and mix it with your red oil, add some gari and your Plantain
  12. Enjoy!!!
