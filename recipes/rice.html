Rice

    Ingredients
  • Rice
  • Salt
  • Oil
  • Onions
  • Water

  1. Fry some sluced onions in your saucepan for some few minutes
  2. Pour your rice in and stir for some 2 minutes
  3. Pour water in it and allow it to cook for 10-15 minutes
  4. Cover the rice with a napkin under the lid when the water has almost finished in it
  5. Leave it for an additional 2 minutes on low heat
  6. Enjoy!!
